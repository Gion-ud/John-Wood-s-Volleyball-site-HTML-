Famous Volleyball Players

Karch Kiraly

Widely regarded as the greatest volleyball player of all time, Karch Kiraly is the only person to have won Olympic gold medals in both indoor and beach volleyball. He was known for his incredible work ethic, court awareness, and leadership. Today, he coaches the U.S. women’s national team.

Giba (Gilberto Godoy Filho)

A Brazilian legend, Giba was known for his energy, charisma, and explosive attacking. He helped Brazil dominate world volleyball in the 2000s, including winning gold at the 2004 Athens Olympics and three World Championships.

Lang Ping

Also known as “The Iron Hammer,” Lang Ping was a star spiker for China in the 1980s and helped them win their first Olympic gold in 1984. She later became a world-renowned coach, winning Olympic gold with China again in 2016—this time as head coach.

Ivan Zaytsev

An Italian player known for his powerful spikes and serves. Zaytsev is also one of the most recognisable faces in modern volleyball due to his athleticism and style of play. He helped Italy secure silver at the 2016 Rio Olympics.

Wilfredo León

Originally from Cuba, Wilfredo León is now a key player for Poland’s national team. He is considered one of the most powerful attackers in the world, with an explosive vertical jump and incredible hitting speed.
